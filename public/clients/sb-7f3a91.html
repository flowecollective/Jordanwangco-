The Aesthetic Brief / SB The Read
// i. The Read

What we know, and what we don't yet

Here is the honest starting point. Right now your hair is carrying volume wefts, and the truth is that we can't fully see what's underneath until they're out. The real density, the condition at the root, where the regrowth actually stands: that's hidden. So this brief doesn't pretend to hand you a finished look. It can't, yet, and I'd rather tell you that than guess.

That's not a weakness in the plan. It is the plan, and you have my word on the non-negotiable you gave me: you are never without hair, not for a single moment of this. Nothing comes down until the same-day coverage is chosen and ready to go on; removal and your new coverage happen together, so there is no bare in-between. And if you'd like to do this part away from the floor, just say the word.

I also want to name the real thing: these wefts have been part of how you've looked and felt for six years. Taking them down isn't a small mechanical step; it touches your identity. So this isn't only about hair on a head; it's about your confidence staying fully intact through every stage. That's the bar I'm holding the whole plan to.

// Where I stop and the doctor starts

Removing the cause of stress on your hair gives your follicles their best chance, but the regrowth question is a medical one, and you're already in good hands there with the dermatologist running your PRP. My role is to build the hair plan around that work, in step with it. That's the line I won't freelance across.

The Aesthetic Brief / SB Phase 01
// ii. Phase 01

Remove & assess

  • Coverage ready first. Before we touch the wefts, your day-one coverage is prepped and on hand: a 100% human-hair Swiss lace wig, undetectable, that no one will read as a wig. It needs no anchor hair and goes on the same day. You leave covered. Every time.
  • Remove the volume wefts. Off comes the weight and tension they've been putting on already-stressed hair, with the new coverage going straight in, so there's no gap.
  • Map your mesh integration. With the wefts off, we finally see the real picture, and that's when we template and customize your full mesh integration to your exact scalp. The wig covers you beautifully while the custom system is built.
  • Coordinate with your dermatologist. You're already doing PRP, so there's no one new to send you to. I'll work alongside your existing derm. The regrowth side stays theirs; the hair stays mine.
// Non-negotiable

You are never without hair. Nothing comes down until that same day's coverage is decided and ready. The questions later are about which direction, never about whether you're covered. That part isn't on the table.

// Private

Off the hard black

You're sitting at a flat, contrast black right now. The single move I'd make is to take it off that hard edge to a soft black, nothing else, no other tone; this is the one we talked through. It's not really a color change, it's a softening: a soft black reads gentler against your skin and lets the light sit on your features instead of bouncing off a wall of black.

// iv. Scalp & Growth Support

The at-home routine

Yours to run between visits, and as an RN, it's well-placed in your hands. The aim is to support the conditions for regrowth, not to promise it.

Lead, growth support
CALECIM Professional · Advanced Hair System
Stem-cell serum + a derma stamper

A non-drug, stem-cell growth-factor treatment you run at home: stamp to activate the scalp, then work in the serum, twice a week. The stamp matters, topically alone the serum doesn't reach the follicle. Results take a few months and vary; this is support, not a guarantee. Because you're also doing PRP, we set this cadence with your dermatologist so we're never stacking stimulation on an injection week.

Optional, strength + body
Milbon Vitalizing Dimension
The luxe pair · shampoo + treatment · keratin + chamomile

The shampoo cleanses and re-energizes; the treatment restores the keratin balance from the inside. This is about the quality and resilience of the hair you have, not regrowth, the finishing layer that makes everything read healthier.

The Aesthetic Brief / SB Sequence
// vi. The Sequence

How it actually runs

Before
Wig prepped. Your 100% human-hair Swiss lace wig is sourced and fitted ahead of time, so it's ready the moment we remove. This is what guarantees you're never without hair.
Day one
Remove, place the wig, map the mesh. Wefts out and the undetectable wig straight on, so you leave covered. With the scalp visible, I template and customize your full mesh integration, and we check in with your derm to align with PRP.
Weeks 1–6
Start the at-home routine. CALECIM stamp + serum on cadence, Zenagen Revolve in the shower, scalp support as needed. Begin measuring honestly.
When it's built
Install the mesh integration, your destination. The custom system goes in once it's ready; the wig stays your flawless backup. Confirm color then (soft black).
Ongoing
Re-assess at 3 months. Coordinate with the medical side, adjust the look as regrowth comes in, maintain.
